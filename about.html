WHO WE ARE

Established in 2001, Millennium Consumer Corporation has served as a cornerstone in the food distribution industry for over two decades. Driven by a singular mission, we strive to bring joy to every Filipino household through high-quality snacks.

OUR LEGACY

History of Sunshine & Expo Peanuts

Expo Peanuts

For years, Expo Peanuts has been a household name for those seeking the perfect crunch. Crafted as a premium coated and greaseless variety, Expo was designed to provide a flavor-packed experience without the mess of traditional oil-fried snacks. It stands as a testament to quality distribution that has spanned over two decades.

Sunshine Green Peas

Sunshine Green Peas brings a burst of flavor to every pack. By focusing on the finest crunchy green and yellow peas, Sunshine has become a staple in Filipino pantries. It represents our legacy of providing snacks that are not only delicious but are part of the tradition of Filipino family bonding.
